Curriculum

Learning Pathways

Foundation Years (Grades 1-5)

Our primary curriculum focuses on building strong foundational skills in literacy and numeracy while encouraging curiosity through play-based learning.

  • Activity-based learning approach
  • Focus on language development
  • Introduction to basic sciences
  • Arts and physical education integration

Exploratory Years (Grades 6-8)

Middle school students transition to more structured learning, exploring a wider range of subjects and developing independent study habits.

  • Project-based learning modules
  • Advanced STEM concepts
  • Third language options
  • Leadership development programs

Preparatory Years (Grades 9-12)

Senior school prepares students for higher education and career paths with specialized streams and rigorous academic standards.

  • Specialized streams (Science, Commerce, Humanities)
  • Career counseling and guidance
  • Competitive exam preparation
  • Research and internship opportunities
